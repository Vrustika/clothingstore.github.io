We are here
to design your dreams

VP boutique is a silk paradise that is home to gorgeous handcrafted pure silk sarees from the beautiful town of kanchipuram. Our ready-made fashion store stands tall in salem with our other branches in kanchipuram, hosur and namakkal. True to the authenticity of the heritage rich kanchipuram silk sarees for wedding, our specializations include cotton sarees, designer sarees, silk cotton sarees, nine yards silk sarees, soft silk sarees and lightweight silk sarees.

Fashion is the armor to survive the reality of everyday life...
